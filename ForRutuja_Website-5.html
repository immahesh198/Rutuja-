The Artemis program is NASA's mission to return humans to the Moon for the first time since Apollo 17 in 1972. Artemis II sends four astronauts on a free-return trajectory around the Moon ‚Äî the farthest crewed journey in over 50 years.

NASA's "Send Your Name to the Moon" campaign allowed people worldwide to register names on a microchip that physically travels to lunar distance aboard the Orion spacecraft.

Rutuja's name is on that chip. When Artemis II launches, her name travels ~385,000 km, loops around the Moon, and returns.
